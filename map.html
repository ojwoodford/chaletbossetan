Morzine is in the heart of the Portes du Soleil ski domain, one of the largest connected ski areas in Europe. It is 8 hours drive from Calais, and an hour drive from Geneva Airport (direct scheduled flights to/from London, Bournemouth, Bristol, East Midlands, Edinburgh, Liverpool, etc.), with regular transfers provided by MorzExpress, Skiidy Gonzales and Morzine Shuttle, amongst others.

Chalet Bossetan is situated in an excellent position just above Morzine, offering great views as well as a short walk to town. You can also ski directly from and back to the chalet, snow conditions permitting.
